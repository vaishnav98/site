Our College

  • Visit

College of Engineering, Trivandrum, commonly known as CET, was founded as the first ever College for Engineering in the state,as a result of the vision of the then Maharajah of Travancore-Sree Chitra Thirunal Balarama Varma. Ever since its inception in 1939, the college has maintained high standards,perpetually maintaining its position as one of the leading engineering colleges in the nation. Extending over 125 acres of land, it has one of the most spacious campuses in the country and is home to the largest technical and cultural fest duo of South India-Drishti and Dhwani respectively.With its level of eminence in academic and curricular activities,great network of Alumni and the quality of its graduates,one does not have to look far to comprehend why CET, with its motto of “Action is superior to Inaction”, is the most sought after engineering college in the state of Kerala.

Our Department

  • Visit

From its humble beginning in 1965 with a total of just 33 students, the Department of Electronics and Communication has grown to its current strength of over 600 students in 2 undergraduate and 4 postgraduate courses. The Golden Jubilee of the department was celebrated during the year 2015, marking 50 years of excellence, and was associated with a year of activities focussed on the technical aspect of engineering education in Kerala. One of the most sought-after departments in the college, our department has a long running track record of producing prestigious alumni, graduates and excellent placements.

Hence, without doubt, we the students of the Department of ECE indeed have a great legacy to follow - the one left by our seniors. We intend to keep the fire burning and aim to raise the bar to even greater heights through a technical festival, celebrating the technological advancements that currently drive the world. An amalgamation of technical and informal events, informative talks, tight competitions and a brilliant networking platform for Electronics students from all over Kerala, the first edition of e-Scape is sure to leave a mark.

About Us

E-scape is meant to be a break, an “escape” from the normal routine of classroom learning. By providing opportunities to make, break and explore through a wide variety of events, this melting pot of ideas will provide the means for students to interact with people outside their classrooms and meet people with contrasting views and vastly different definitions of creativity.

Planned are plenty of talks that give students insights into the whats, hows and whys of the latest developments in technology, ensuring that they are up-to-date and well informed of current events in the world of their technical domain. Our workshops are also sure to leave students all the more geekier. Also in store for participants are contests and challenges to prove their mettle and win exciting prizes. Last, but definitely not the least, what better way to end the day than with the array of informals that we have planned out?
